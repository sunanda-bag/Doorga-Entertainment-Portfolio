About Us







Who are we?


Shuga is an acronym for Sophisticated Humanitarian United Genius Alliance, also when salty sea water hits cool temperatures making white ice, a Shuga is formed, similar to my Trini roots like the ocean meshing with my cool Canadian flavor. It just seemed like the perfect name.

Together with my Mom, brother and sister in law, we started out 12 years ago as a print magazine in a few countries but after I moved to Trinidad to pursue my career in Media working on Radio, TV and Print media, there was a fire that destroyed everything and Shuga was put on hold. As the years went by we planned to relaunch several times and had an extensive and powerful network spanning all over North America Guyana and Trinidad, but, it just wasn't the right timing and life changing events took place including becoming a mom, and the passing of both my parents and going back to school and a really bad accident that left me with only 40% use of my dominant hand. That wasn't gonna stop me. My son Aryan and nieces Veda and Sitara were my inspiration to show them that in the face of adversity you can still rise above it, just have faith in yourself. Finally, 6 months after my accident, in 2021, I decided to relaunch Shuga as a docuseries. We decided to go from print to virtually visual because we saw that people didn't Have to much time to spend reading or watching TV.

With a docuseries ranging from 5-8 mins we could make an impact with a good amount of information in the persons own words relayed in a more comfortable and relaxed manner. We highlight the understated voices and unsung heros that are making movements and not always recognized for it. We chose only a few topics that highlight the featured guest on a more personal level. Our main goal is to humanize them so that they are relatable. By sharing some of their struggles and accomplishments, it may give hope to some who may be in a similar situation and possibly bring awareness on issues otherwise swept under the carpet.
The Vlogs can be found on Facebook under the Shuga Magazine and Doorga Entertainment Pages. You will also be able to find us on Instagram and YouTube.
